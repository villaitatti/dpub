"This Exhibition presents the extant correspondence - nearly all unpublished - between Bernard Berenson and Yukio Yashiro. At the height of their fame they were among the most influential art historians in the West and in Japan, respectively." [...] (read more)

The correspondence is available in the Letters section. Alternatively, you can read the introduction by Michiaki Koshikawa.

This collection also hosts digital editions of Texts by Yashiro and studies on yashiro.
